Salarpuria Sattva Cadenza Overview

Salarpuria Sattva Cadenza, our newest residential project on Hosur Main Road at Kudlu Junction. Just 4 km from Silk Board and 2 km from the Greenage residential project. It is en route to the Electronic city and located in the middle of job hubs, well developed micro markets and social infrastructure. It has quick connectivity through the expressway, Ring Road and NICE Road to everything that matters in Bangalore city.
